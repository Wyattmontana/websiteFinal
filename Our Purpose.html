Master Data Initiative

Under the BI&A team, we are building a robust, central-led and governed Master Data Organization, with dedicated roles and clear responsibilities under the leadersip of Jo O'Hanzo, VP of Enterprise Data. This orgainzation is responsbile for the following :

  • Improve the management and accuracy of Master Data across key domains which include Product, Customer, Employee, Finance, and Consumer
  • Deliver improved management of Master Data with enhanced data accuracy and accessibility thorugh data cleansing, process standardization, automation, organizational accountability and increased foucs on operational excellence and continuous improvement
  • Enable critical data access by delivering the single version of truth across the Enterprixe and powering analytics and delivering insights

Approach

  • Clean Data (including Quality Control Dashboard)supporting E2E processes across domains
  • Establish Organization Model focused on stewardship and accountability for data across functions & processes
  • Implement robust Online, Workflow / Automation Tools to speed up activities & enable 1st time right
  • Establish Single Version of Truth (SVOT) which will enable Analytics and self-service content
